Welcome to Lodwar County Referral Hospital (LCRH)

offering services that address a range of medical needs from primary care to specialized interventions.
The hospital also works to serve the underserved population in the region, ensuring access to healthcare
in one of Kenya's more remote areas.

ABOUT LCRH TURKANA

Lodwar County Referral Hospital (LCRH) is the largest and most advanced public health facility in Turkana County, located in Lodwar Town, the county headquarters. As a Level 5 referral hospital, LCRH serves as the primary center for specialized healthcare in the region, catering to patients from across Turkana and neighboring counties, including parts of West Pokot and Samburu, as well as cross-border communities from South Sudan and Ethiopia.
